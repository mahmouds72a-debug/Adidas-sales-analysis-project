# Adidas-sales-analysis-project
# Business Problem
Performance is uneven across regions, sales channels, product categories, and retailers.
# Tools Used
- Excel
- Power query
- Pivot tables
- Pivot charts
# Key Insights
- The West region generates the highest sales and The Midwest is the lowest-performing region.
- In-store is the dominant channel and Outlet is the weakest channel.
- Street Footwear has the highest share.
- West Gear and Foot Locker are top-performing retailers but amazon and walmart the lowest.
- Strong growth observed in 2021 compared to 2020.
# Recommendations
- Analyze the success factors behind the West region and implement similar strategies in underperforming regions like the Midwest.
- Improve digital marketing and user experience.
- Invest further in the online channel to increase conversion and customer reach.
- Increase promotional efforts for Athletic Footwear.
- Introduce incentive-based performance programs.
